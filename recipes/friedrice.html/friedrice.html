Nigerian fried rice recipe

Fried rice is another of my favorite Nigerian food. I like it because it contains lots of healthy ingredients and it's quite easy to prepare.

Most people prepare this food only during a special occassion because it can be quiet expensive especially if you want to add all the major ingredients that makes this meal mouth irrestitible.

Nigerian Fried Rice Ingredients

Below are the ingredients for this meal. Note that it is not compulsory to use all of the vegetables listed

  • White rice (I like to use the Basmati rice but feel free to use any rice of choice
  • Vegetable or coconut oil
  • Spring Onion, carrot, bell pepper (green, red and yellow)
  • Sweet corn, green pea
  • Liver
  • Onion
  • Salt and seasoning cubes
  • Crayfish (optional)
  • Yellow habanero pepper
  • Curry and thyme
  • Meat of choice (I like to use turkey)

Preparation Guide

  1. Parboil your rice, wash and set aside
  2. Wash, season and cook your meat (Including the liver) till done
  3. Wash all the vegetables thoroughly under running water and cut as desired
  4. Once your meat is cooked, bring it down and fry or bake if you want
  5. Next set a clean pot on the fire, add oil to the dried pot, allow to heat and then add the sliced onion and allow to simmer for 2mins
  6. Add the blended crayfish if you are using some, also add the curry and thyme powder and stir for 2mins
  7. Pour in the meat stock at this point, stir and taste for salt
  8. Bring the pot to a boil and add your parboiled rice and stirly properly, cover the pot and allow to cook
  9. While the rice is cooking, stir fry your vegetables with a little vegetable oil (start with the carrot and green pea first because they are harder than other vegetables)
  10. Once your rice is well cooked and dry, pour in the stir fried veggies and stir evenly
  11. Turn off the heat and serve hot
