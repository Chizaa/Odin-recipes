Yam and Egg Sauce Recipe

Yam and egg sauce is one meal that is super fast to prepare and can serve as breakfast, lunch or dinner

You can count on this meal when you are exhausted from the day's work and want a quick fix in the kitchen

Yam and Egg Sauce Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Eggs
  • Fresh Tomatoes and pepper
  • Onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • salt and seasoning cubes
  • (Optional) Green veggies like spring onion and green bell pepper)

Preparation Guide

  1. Peel your yam, place in a bowl of cold water and set aside
  2. Wash and cut your veggies
  3. Place a pot of water in the fire and add the yam, add salt to taste and allow to cook
  4. While your yam is cooking, place your fry pan in another fire, let it dry then add the oil and allow to heat a bit
  5. Add the sliced onion and stir for 2mins
  6. Add the fresh tomatoes and pepper and allow the extra water to dry leaving only oil in the pot
  7. Add the eggs (Plus other veggies if available). Let it sit for 2mins then stir
  8. Your egg sauce is ready. I'm sure your yam is soft by now.
  9. Serve serve serve....
