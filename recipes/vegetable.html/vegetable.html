Vegetable Soup Recipe

Vegetable soup is a delicious meal popularly eaten by Nigerians especially the Igbo and Calabar people.

The soup can be eaten with any swallow of choice or with rice or even yam.

The soup is my favorite Nigerian soups because it is highly nutritious as its major ingredients are fresh vegetables such as ugu (Pumpkin leaves) and waterleaf

Vegetable soup ingredients

Below are the ingredients for making this sumptious meal

(Prep time: 45mins-1hr)

  • Ugu (Pumpkin leaf)
  • Waterleaf
  • Meat (Goat meat, beef or turkey)
  • Soft cow skin
  • Palm oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Blended Crayfish
  • Smoked fish of choice. (I prefer smoked catfish)
  • Habenero pepper
  • Onion
  • Salt to taste

Preparation guide

  1. Wash your meat thoroughly and season with seasoning cubes, salt and onion and cook till done
  2. While your meat is cooking, wash and slice your vegetables and set aside
  3. Wash the smoked fish with some hot water and set aside (Take off the bone if necessary)
  4. Wash with hot water and Cut the cowskin into chewable parts or as desired
  5. Check your meat and ensure it's soft then add palm oil to the boiling pot (Make sure the meat stock is little because the soup doesn't require much water)
  6. Add the washed smoked fish and cowskin and allow to boil for 5mins
  7. Check for taste and add more salt and seasoning cubes if needed and allow to boil for 2mins
  8. Now add the waterleaf and blended crayfish and pepper, stir and allow to cook for 2mins
  9. Next add the pumpkin leaf, stir and check for taste
  10. Allow to cook for additional 3mins and turn off the heat
    11. Voila! your delicious vegetable soup is ready to serve.
